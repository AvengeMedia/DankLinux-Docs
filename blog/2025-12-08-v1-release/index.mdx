We're excited to announce DankMaterialShell (DMS) v1.0! What started as a small hobby project has grown into a full desktop shell and application suite with thousands of users and dozens of contributors.

This release marks more than just a version number—it's a commitment to stability and predictability. Going forward, DMS will follow a bi-monthly release cycle, with bug-fix releases as needed, as we continue to mature the project and expand the community and ecosystem.
